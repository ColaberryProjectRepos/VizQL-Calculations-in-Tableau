Taxes of each Region

Drag and drop Region on column shelves. 

Click Analysis then click Create Calculated Field. 


Write calculated field name Employee and syntax SUM ([Employee Count]) then click ok. 


Drag and drop Employee on the row shelves and Region on the color.
